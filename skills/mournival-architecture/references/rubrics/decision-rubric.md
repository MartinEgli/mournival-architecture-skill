# Decision Rubric

Use this rubric to convert review findings into action.

## Checkpoints

- Is there a decision need?
- Is priority clear?
- Is the process blocked?
- Is human review required?
- Is there a go/no-go recommendation?
- Are required actions concrete?

## Verdict Guidance

- `accepted`: no relevant decision blocker.
- `accepted_with_conditions`: can proceed after explicit conditions.
- `blocked`: process, evidence, risk, or pipeline state prevents next step.
- `requires_human_review`: accountable human decision needed.
- `rejected`: option should not continue.

## Hard Veto

Block when:

- required review is missing
- pipeline state is not approved
- high-risk decision is being accepted autonomously
- productive use is requested while Assurance or Risk blocks
