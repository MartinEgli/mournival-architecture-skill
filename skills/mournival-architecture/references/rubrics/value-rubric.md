# Value Rubric

Use this rubric to judge usability and stakeholder value.

## Checkpoints

- Is the target audience clear?
- Is the benefit clear?
- Is the artifact actionable?
- Is structure suitable for use?
- Is complexity appropriate?
- Is a management summary needed?
- Are examples or definitions missing?

## Usability Score

- `0.0-0.3`: unusable for intended purpose.
- `0.31-0.6`: partly usable; major rework needed.
- `0.61-0.8`: usable with improvements.
- `0.81-1.0`: usable and clear.

## Hard Veto

Block only when the artifact cannot be used for its intended purpose even if it
is technically correct.

## Soft Veto

Recommend rework when clarity, audience fit, examples, or actionability are weak
but evidence and risk allow continued work.
