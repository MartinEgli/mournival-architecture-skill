# Review Knowledge Claim

Use when reviewing a Knowledge Claim or Knowledge Claim Candidate.

## Required Checks

- Identify claim text.
- Identify source reference.
- Identify fragment or conversation excerpt reference.
- Identify evidence class.
- Check whether wording overstates evidence.
- Check productive-use eligibility.
- Identify human confirmation need.

## Steward Questions

Decision: Should the claim be accepted, reworked, escalated, or rejected?

Risk: What happens if the claim is wrong or too strong?

Value: Is the claim understandable, useful, and reusable?

Assurance: Is the claim sufficiently supported and traceable?
