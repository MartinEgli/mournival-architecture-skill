# Review Artifact

Use when reviewing architecture documents, decision records, guidance, diagrams,
RAG candidates, or training dataset candidates.

## Required Checks

- Identify artifact purpose and target audience.
- Check source binding and traceability for important claims.
- Check data classification and productive-use risk.
- Check usability and management readability.
- Check whether final decision can be made.

## Steward Questions

Decision: Can the artifact proceed, with which conditions?

Risk: Which side effects or misuse paths exist?

Value: Is the artifact usable by the intended audience?

Assurance: Are evidence, version, and execution context sufficient?
